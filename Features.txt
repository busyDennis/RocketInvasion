GAME FEAURES
===============================================================================================
ALIEN SPRITES

Aliens are either dormant and sitting in the hive or attacking.

Attack by individual alien:
(1) Alien starts with a predefined direction and constantly steers to the left and to the right
with a varying angular speed.
(2) Alien launches bombs.
(3) After disappearing below the low border of the screen alien returns to its original location
in the hive from the top of the screen.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
INFO PANEL

Shows number of lives and a score.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ANIMAIONS

- Animated sprites, including rocket engine flame for player sprite.
- Animated background.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
DEVICE COMPATIBILITY

- Sprite size proportions for various devices.
